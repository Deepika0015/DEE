Introduction

HTML Stands for HyperText Markup Language.
It is standard Language used to create and design web pages on the internet.
It was introduced by Tim berners-Lee in 1991 at CERN as a simple Markup language.


Advantages:

1.Easy to learn and use.
2.Supported by all Brousers.
3.Simple syntax.
4.SEO-Friendly.
5.Platform indepentent.


Disadvantages:

1.Limited sesign options.
2.Lack of interactivity.
3.Offline browsing is not supported.
4.Difficulty in complex programming tasks.
5.Security vulnerabilities.
